License Information

Generally, the license is issued for each individual ship per year. Vessel name and IMO Number are pre-fed value when the license is procured. If the Vessel name and / or IMO number is amended, kindly request your Office to liaison with Work-Ship Support Team to renew the details by means of an update.


Extending License before Validity Expires


To extend your software license validity, please login via Admin / Master id, click on menu - Administration > License and follow below procedure. Click on "Renew License" hyperlink to open a pop-up, enter your new License key to extend the Validity of the Software



To obtain a New License Key, request your Office to send a request to Work-Ship Support Team.



Extending License after Validity Expires


Once your license validity expires, Work-ship will not allow any user to Login. You will see a Validity Expired Warning on the login Screen and a place to enter the new License key.

To obtain a New License Key, request your Office to send a request to Work-Ship Support Team.
