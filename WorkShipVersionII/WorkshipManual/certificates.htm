General Introduction

Certificates Module comes as a Free Add-on to this Software License. If expiry date and survey due dates of any certificates are approaching then system will generate a Notification alert. This message will be based on alert frequency of certificate that Administrator must have set whilst entering the certificate details.







Add/Edit/Delete Certificate

From the MAIN MENU, select Certificate

To enter the certificate detail click on Add Certificate button.


To create a New Certificate, use following steps -


  1. Click on "Add Certificate" button, which will open a Pop-up Window as above.
  2. In Add Certificate Pop-up window, type Certificate Name (eg: Cargo ship safety construction certificate(SAFCON)) and following details -
    • Date of Issue : Insert date using calendar
    • Date of Expiry : Insert date using calendar
    • Next Survey Due Date : Insert date using calendar
    • Remarks : Text field, for general remarks pertaining to the respective Certificate
    • Alert Frequency for Expiry Date > Multi-select checkbox, check the option for desired duration of alert frequency in Certificate Notifications Window
    • Alert Frequency for Survey Due Date > Multi-select checkbox, check the option for desired duration of alert frequency in Certificate Notifications Window
    • After filling above information, click on "Save" button to Save all information or click on "Cancel" button if information filled is not required to be saved.

  3. To Edit a Certificate Details which is already inserted*, Click on "Edit" Hyperlink, which will opena Pop-up Window with the present Certificate details, edit with new details and Click on "Update" button.
  4. To Delete a Certificate & its details, which is already inserted, Click on "Delete" Hyperlink, which will open a Pop-up Confirmation Window, press "Yes" button to Delete.



Sorting Order

Once you have entered all the details of all certificates, you will find the list of certificates. You can also arrange the serial order by simply Drag and Drop row and click on save order button.

Managing Sorting Order of "Certificate" list
This list can be sorted by Simply left click + Hold & Drag Symbol button to the desired Vertical location, Press "Save Order" button after re-locating the positions, at last. By Default, newly added Certificates show at the bottom of the list.



Export List/Save List/Print List

You can also export and print the list of certificate by click on Export List button.

To Export the Certificate List in Excel or PDF Format, click on "Export List" button, then click on respective option, the software will download the desired extension file.
To Print the Certificate list, click on "Export List" button, then click on print option, the software will open the printer window to choose from available printers & print.



If expiry date and survey due date of certificate are approaching near then you will get alert message about certificate on the taskbar. This message will be based on alert frequency of certificate.
