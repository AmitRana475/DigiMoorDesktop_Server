Update Licence

Generally, the license is issued for each individual ship per year. Vessel name and IMO Number are pre-fed value when the license is procured. If the Vessel name and / or IMO number is amended, kindly request your Office to liaison with Work-Ship Support Team to renew the details by means of an update.

Once your license validity expires, Work-ship will not allow any user to Login. You will see a Validity Expired Warning on the login Screen and a place to enter the new License key. To obtain a New License Key, request your Office to send a request to Work-Ship Support Team.
