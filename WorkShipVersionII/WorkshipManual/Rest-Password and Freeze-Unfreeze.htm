Reset Password

From the MAIN MENU, select Crew Management

From this SUB MENU, select Reset Password


Admin and Master can change password of any crew member from Reset Password. HOD can also change password from his department's crew member if have access permission to HOD.



Freeze-Unfreeze

From the MAIN MENU, click Crew Management

From this SUB MENU, click Freeze-Unfreeze


Admin and Master can freeze or unfreeze Past and future attendance marking of All crew member from Freeze-Unfreeze. HOD can also freeze or unfreeze Past and future attendance marking from his department's crew member if have access permission to HOD. It has also feature of auto-freeze the last previous month record after the 7 day of current month.
