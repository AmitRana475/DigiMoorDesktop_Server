About Work-Ship

Work-Ship is a state-of-art computer based program, intended for use onboard ships and in offices to monitor the Vessel's Crew Work and Rest hours. This Software assists in maintaining the individual work and rest hours of the crew onboard ships, in compliance with all the current International rules & regulations via, STCW-2010, MLC-2006 and OPA-90.This software which has been developed by 49webstreet, aids in construing and complying with the applicable international laws in a very harmonious and simplified way which makes it very easy to use and predict the future non-compliance as well as deviations which might seep in based on the normal work routine of the seafarer; having known the possible future deviations, The Head of Department now has a magnified and crystal clear view as to the availability of the man-power at his disposal and can plan out efficiently to avoid the induction of fatigue to crew members under him. Work-Ship provides the most ergonomic User-Interface. The latest Version of Work-Ship, X.X, boasts itself of the following new and fresh features, including but not limited to:-

  1. Overtime of the crew, if applicable
  2. Comprehensive network security layers, User-level access permissions & secure data storage.
  3. Real-time Notification system for HOD & Master

Add-On Features -

  1. Record keeping of all the Vessel's Statutory, mandatory and miscellaneous certificates

Work-Ship makes the compliance with all international laws and regulations look very easy, as it has never been before !!!
