License

License window manifests the Licensee's information on whom the product is registered. Generally, the license is issued for each individual ship per year.


The fields are for display only, non-editable

To edit any information, request your Office to send a request to Work-Ship Support Team.

Extend License : Click on "Renew License" hyperlink to open a pop-up, enter your new License key to extend the Validity of the Software


To obtain a New License Key, request your Office to send a request to Work-Ship Support Team.
