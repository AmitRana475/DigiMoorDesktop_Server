Freeze-Unfreeze

Freeze-Unfreeze Only applicable to Crew members other than Master & Admin, unless permission granted to HODs.

The Software will “Auto-Freeze” data as per set frequency of days from the setting as in screenshot. No crew member will be able to insert/edit/delete their work hours for the frozen data.

For eg:- If today is 04th March 2017 and automatic freezing frequency is set to 2 Days, no crew will be able to insert/edit/delete their work hours for any date on and before 02nd March 2017.


However, there is an additional procedure to unfreeze / freeze data manually, as follows –
Admin, Master and/or HOD (with permission) can freeze or unfreeze desired date values for All Crew :

Manual Freeze: All Crew will not be able to insert/edit/delete any work hours records, for the chosen period. This will override the auto-freeze function.

Unfreeze: All Crew Work & Rest hours will be opened to make changes for the chosen period. This will override the auto-freeze function. .

To Freeze or Unfreeze values, select "From" & "To" dates and press respective "Unfreeze" or "Freeze" button, which will be followed by a successful prompt.
